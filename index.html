Our Ongoing Projects

Our flagship initiatives focus on creating sustainable social impact through education, community service, environmental protection, women empowerment, youth development, and animal welfare.

Seva

Seva is dedicated to helping underprivileged individuals and families by providing food, clothing, and other essential necessities. Through regular donation drives and community support initiatives, the project spreads compassion and hope to those who need it most.

Bachpanshala

Bachpanshala focuses on providing quality education, learning resources, and care for underprivileged children, helping them build confidence and create brighter futures through knowledge and opportunity.

Jeev

Jeev promotes kindness towards animals by supporting rescue efforts, providing food, encouraging adoption, and spreading awareness about animal welfare and responsible care.

Udaan

Udaan empowers women through skill development, education, mentorship, and opportunities that encourage financial independence, confidence, and long-term growth.

Prakriti

Prakriti is committed to environmental conservation through plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, sustainability awareness, and encouraging communities to protect nature for future generations.

Vikas

Vikas helps students and young professionals enhance their skills through internships, leadership opportunities, mentorship, and practical learning experiences that prepare them for successful careers.

Our Social Impact

Every initiative at InAmigos Foundation is driven by the belief that collective action can transform lives. By addressing education, environmental sustainability, women empowerment, community welfare, animal care, and youth development, the foundation continues to create meaningful and lasting social impact.

With the support of dedicated volunteers, interns, and partners, InAmigos has built a growing community committed to making positive change across society. More than 250 interns have become a permanent part of the InAmigos family, reflecting the organization's commitment to empowering individuals while serving communities.
